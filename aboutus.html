ABOUT US

AMBIKA VIDYABHAVAN: “The tradition of excellence is ours; the choice is yours!”

Ambika Vidyabhavan is located off state highway SH-8 between Pala and Thodupuzha at Aimcompu, a serene small village in Meenachil Taluk, Kottayam District. Established in 1982, the school holds a much-acclaimed academic portfolio along with cultural and quality aspects and a forthright adherence to the ethics and philosophy of Indian Cultural Heritage.
Ambika Educational Charitable Trust is in the process of transforming the school into an outstanding Residential Junior College to meet the global challenges propounded by the world of education and prepare our young generation to face up to it. Ambika is associated to Bharatheeya Vidya Nikethan (BVN), the Kerala chapter of ‘Vidyabharathi’, the single largest educational agency in the world with more than 40,000 educational institutions. Founded in 1979, in order to formulate and develop national education in Kerala, BVN has more than 400 institutions till date.
