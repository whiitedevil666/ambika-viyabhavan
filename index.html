WELCOME

Our mission at Ambika Vidyabhavan is simple and straight forward whereby we are committed to create a profound as well as pragmatic learning environment securing the future for our students.We provide high standards of learning within a rich and varied school curriculum.Our rural setting enables our children to enjoy learning outside the classroom, whilst our committed staff and active parent community support a range of activities and visits.

We value the support of our active Parent Teacher Association, a group of parents who work very hard to raise extra funding for our pupils so that they are able to enjoy additional opportunities that come their way. Although our traditions are never far from the surface, an ongoing program for improvements reveals that our school has a very forward looking ideology based on the goodness of retrospective thinking. There is something very special about us; everybody who walks through our school notices how amazing our children are. They are beautifully behaved, well-mannered and aspirational.
